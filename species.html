About This Data

Parameters are scorer estimates calibrated against published behavioral and neuroscience evidence. HIGH confidence means published data supports most parameters. MEDIUM means partial data with inference. LOW means mostly extrapolated from related species. Tier boundaries are estimated working thresholds (Self-Aware ≥50%, Conscious ≥18%, Subconscious ≥5%, Reactive ≥1%, Minimal <1% of human waking awareness). This database is a work in progress and will be revised as empirical data accumulates.
